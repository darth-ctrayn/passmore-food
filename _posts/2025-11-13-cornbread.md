# Cornbread
---

Makes one 9-inch round cake pan or roughly an 8x8 square pan

---

## Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 400 F. Mix the dry ingredients, and the wet seperately, then mix together.

Grease the pan, cook for 25 mins. The cornbread should be golden brown and a fork should come out clean after poking it in the center.
